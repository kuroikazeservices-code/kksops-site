Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how KuroiKaze Service Operations ("KKSops," "we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our mobile applications and this website.

1. Information we collect

Our apps are designed to work with data you enter directly — such as client names, contact details, and invoice information. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

We may collect the following through standard app and website operation:

  • Account information you provide when creating an account (if applicable)
  • App usage data and crash reports to improve performance and fix bugs
  • Device information such as operating system version and app version

We do not collect payment card information. Any in-app purchases are processed by the platform (Google Play or Apple App Store) directly.

2. How we use information

Information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve our products

3. Data sharing and disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in app operation (e.g., crash reporting tools), bound by confidentiality agreements
  • If required by law or valid legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of KKSops or its users

4. Data retention

Data stored locally on your device remains under your control and can be deleted by uninstalling the app. If you contact us for support, we retain that correspondence only as long as necessary to resolve your request.

5. Children's privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
